Hip-Hop began in the early 1970s by DJ Kool Herc.
Hip-Hop is not just a music genre, it is a culture. This culture has given rise to individuals that were considered worthless, or never taken seriously as having anything valuable to contribute to society. Hip-Hop expresses the pain, the joy, the journeys of many generations. The creativity of the culture has spawned its own diverse lexicon, community, style, sound, artwork, dance, poetry, and activists.
